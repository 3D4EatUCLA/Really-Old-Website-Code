3D4E at UCLA

3D4E (3D For Everyone) is an intercollegiate network for enthusiasts of 3D printing and rapid-prototyping. There are currently four chapters in the network, founded by students at UCLA, Univeristy of Souther California, Loyola Marymount University, and San Diego State University.

In Fall 2014, 3D4E at UCLA co-hosted a 3D printing exhibition with Young's Research Library to demonstrate the capabilities of 3D printers. The event attracted the interest of more than 200 people and greatly increased the awareness of 3D printing on campus. In Spring 2015, we secured a sponsorship agreement with 3D printer manufacturer XYZPrinting Inc. Other partners include StartUP UCLA, a hub on campus for up and coming technologies and businesses, and the Blackstone LaunchPad, where we house our 3D printers and hose workshops and events.
